FAQs

Have Questions? Find your answers from the FAQs given below:

How do I make a donation?

  • You can make a donation on our website by clicking on the "Donate" button and following the prompts. You can choose to make a one-time donation or set up a recurring donation.

Will I receive updates on how my donation is being used?

  • Yes, we will keep you informed about the impact of your donation through periodic email updates, newsletters, and annual reports. If you would like more frequent updates or have specific questions about our programs, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

  • Yes, we are a registered nonprofit organization, and your donation is tax-deductible to the fullest extent allowed by law. You will receive a tax receipt via email for your records.
